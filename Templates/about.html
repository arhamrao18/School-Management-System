Our Story

Two Decades of Growth

From a small school with a handful of classrooms to a full-fledged institution with modern facilities — our journey has been one of constant progress and dedication.

2005
Founded

GV School was established with 3 classrooms, 12 teachers, and 120 students.

2010
Secondary Wing Added

Expanded to include Classes VII–X with a dedicated science lab and library.

2015
Digital Classrooms

Introduced smart boards and computer labs. Student count crossed 800.

2020
Online Learning During COVID

Seamlessly shifted to online classes with zero interruption to academic year.

2024
Digital Management System

Launched GV School Management System — full digitization of admin, teachers, and students.
